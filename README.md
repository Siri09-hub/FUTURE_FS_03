# ☕ BlueWave Café & Bistro – Business Website

A modern and premium **business website** built for a real-world café to enhance its online presence, attract customers, and showcase services in a visually appealing way.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

BlueWave Café & Bistro website is designed to represent a café professionally on the internet. It helps customers explore the menu, view services, and easily contact or locate the café.

This project focuses on real-world usability, clean design, and customer engagement.

---

## 🏪 Business Details

- **Business Name:** BlueWave Café & Bistro  
- **Location:** Bhimavaram  
- **Type:** Café / Restaurant  

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this website is to:

- Create a strong online presence  
- Attract new customers  
- Showcase menu and services  
- Improve customer engagement  
- Provide easy contact and location access  

---

## 🍽️ Services Offered

- ☕ Coffee (Espresso, Cappuccino, Latte)  
- 🍹 Mocktails & Fresh Juices  
- 🍔 Fast Food (Burgers, Sandwiches, Pizza)  
- 🍰 Desserts (Cakes, Brownies, Ice Cream)  
- 🪑 Comfortable hangout space
